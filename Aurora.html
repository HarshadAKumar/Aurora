Government Collab

following any kind of sexual abuse. According to Article 75, the offender may receive a maximum sentence

pain that the child is experiencing because they are actually fighting just to survive while the brains of

tighten the regulations to the point where the individual has to think atleast twice before carrying out such
heinous acts. For instance, the government took 7 years 3 months and 4 days to give the final verdict on the
